==================================================
AI CREDIT SYSTEM AND ADD-ON PACKS
=================================

IMPORTANT

OpenAI API billing remains centralized under the CRM owner's OpenAI account.

Customers do NOT receive individual OpenAI accounts.

Customers purchase CRM AI usage allowances.

The system must track and enforce usage internally.

==================================================
DATABASE
========

Create table:

organization_addons

Fields:

* id
* organization_id
* addon_type
* quantity
* quantity_remaining
* purchased_at
* expires_at
* created_at
* updated_at

Supported addon_type values:

* ai_requests
* leads
* storage
* whatsapp_accounts

==================================================
AI USAGE CALCULATION
====================

Available AI Requests

=

Plan Included Requests

*

Remaining AI Add-On Requests

When an AI action executes:

1. Deduct from included plan allowance first
2. Then deduct from purchased add-ons
3. Log usage

==================================================
AI LIMIT REACHED
================

When no AI requests remain:

Block all AI functionality.

Display:

"You have reached your monthly AI limit."

Provide actions:

* Upgrade Plan
* Purchase Additional AI Requests

==================================================
MONTHLY RESET
=============

On monthly billing reset:

Reset:

* Included AI Requests

Do NOT reset:

* Purchased AI Add-On Requests

Purchased AI Add-On Requests remain available until consumed or expired.
