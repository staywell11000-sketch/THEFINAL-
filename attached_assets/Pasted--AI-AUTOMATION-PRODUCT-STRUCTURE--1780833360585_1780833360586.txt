==================================================
AI & AUTOMATION PRODUCT STRUCTURE
=================================

IMPORTANT

AI Intelligence and Automations are separate modules.

AI Intelligence provides:

* Analysis
* Recommendations
* Insights
* Summaries

Automations provide:

* Actions
* Scheduled Tasks
* Trigger-Based Workflows
* Follow-Up Sequences

Do not mix these modules.

==================================================
AI INTELLIGENCE
===============

The AI Intelligence module is responsible for helping users understand their business and make better decisions.

AI Intelligence Features:

1. AI Lead Scoring

* Hot Lead
* Warm Lead
* Cold Lead

Analyze:

* Budget
* Engagement
* Messages
* Activity
* Deal Stage

---

2. AI Lead Summaries

Generate concise lead summaries.

Examples:

* Budget
* Interests
* Property Requirements
* Conversation Summary
* Recommended Action

---

3. AI Reply Suggestions

Inside WhatsApp conversations:

Generate:

* Suggested Reply 1
* Suggested Reply 2
* Suggested Reply 3

Allow:

* Copy
* Edit
* Send

---

4. AI Deal Insights

Analyze:

* Conversion Probability
* Deal Risk
* Opportunity Strength
* Follow-Up Priorities

---

5. AI Business Insights

Generate:

* Daily Business Summary
* Weekly Business Summary
* Monthly Business Summary

Examples:

* Leads Received
* Deals Closed
* Top Agent
* Top Campaign
* Conversion Trends

---

6. AI Risk Detection

Identify:

* Uncontacted Leads
* Cold Leads
* Stalled Deals
* Missed Opportunities

---

7. AI Property Matching

Match:

* Lead Requirements
* Budget
* Location
* Plot Size

with available properties.

---

8. AI Analytics

Display:

* AI Usage
* Cost Tracking
* Requests Used
* Token Usage
* Most Used AI Features

==================================================
AUTOMATIONS
===========

Automations are responsible for performing actions automatically.

==================================================
AUTOMATION FEATURES
===================

1. Lead Assignment

Trigger:

New Lead Created

Actions:

* Assign Agent
* Notify Agent

---

2. WhatsApp Welcome Messages

Trigger:

New Facebook Lead

Actions:

* Send Approved WhatsApp Template

---

3. Follow-Up Workflows

Trigger Examples:

* No Response After 24 Hours
* No Response After 3 Days
* Site Visit Scheduled
* Deal Stage Changed

Actions:

* Generate AI Follow-Up
* Send WhatsApp Message
* Create Task
* Notify Agent

---

4. Appointment Reminders

Trigger:

Appointment Upcoming

Actions:

* WhatsApp Reminder
* Email Reminder
* CRM Notification

---

5. Lead Nurturing Campaigns

Examples:

* New Lead Sequence
* Buyer Sequence
* Seller Sequence
* Investor Sequence

---

6. Re-Engagement Campaigns

Target:

Inactive Leads

Actions:

* WhatsApp Follow-Up
* Email Follow-Up
* Task Creation

---

7. Workflow Builder

Allow users to create workflows using:

Triggers

Conditions

Actions

Examples:

IF Lead Source = Facebook

THEN Send Welcome Message

IF Lead Not Contacted For 24 Hours

THEN Notify Agent

==================================================
UPGRADE STRATEGY
================

Professional Plan is designed to introduce users to AI.

Professional users should experience:

* AI Summaries
* AI Reply Suggestions

These features are expected to drive upgrades from Starter.

Agency Plan should contain the most advanced and expensive AI capabilities:

* AI Chatbot
* AI Automations
* Workflow Builder
* Advanced Intelligence

This creates a natural upgrade path:

Free
→ Starter
→ Professional
→ Agency

# while maximizing conversion rates and controlling AI costs.
