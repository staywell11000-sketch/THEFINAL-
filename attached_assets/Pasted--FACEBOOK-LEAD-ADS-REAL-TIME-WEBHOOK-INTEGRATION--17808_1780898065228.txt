WEBHOOK VERIFICATION
====================

GET /api/facebook/webhook

Handle Meta verification requests.

Meta will send:

hub.mode
hub.verify_token
hub.challenge

Logic:

If verify token matches:

FACEBOOK_WEBHOOK_VERIFY_TOKEN

Return:

hub.challenge

Otherwise:

403 Forbidden
