IMPORTANT

The current Team Management implementation is incorrect.

It appears every account is being treated as an employee account.

This is NOT the intended SaaS architecture.

Do not continue building on the current employee-first model.

Refactor the system to follow the architecture below.

==================================================
CORRECT ACCOUNT STRUCTURE
==================================================

When a person signs up for the CRM:

They become:

Organization Owner

NOT Employee.

Flow:

User Registers
↓
Organization Created
↓
User Becomes Organization Owner
↓
User Gets Full CRM Access
↓
User Can Invite Employees

==================================================
ORGANIZATION OWNER
==================================================

The person who purchases the CRM plan is:

Organization Owner

Permissions:

- Full CRM Access
- Billing Access
- Subscription Access
- Team Management
- Employee Management
- WhatsApp Connections
- Facebook Connections
- Settings Access
- Permission Management

Owner should never be treated as an employee.

Owner is the top-level account inside the organization.

==================================================
EMPLOYEE SYSTEM
==================================================

Employees must only exist inside an organization.

Employees do NOT create organizations.

Employees do NOT purchase subscriptions.

Employees do NOT own CRM accounts.

Employees belong to an existing organization.

Structure:

Organization
 ├── Owner
 ├── Manager
 ├── Sales Agent
 ├── Marketing User
 └── Custom Roles

==================================================

==================================================

Team
→ Add Employee

- Full Name
- Email
- Position
- Role
- Permissions

Employee receives:

- Invite Email

- Invite Code

Employee joins the existing organization.

Employee should never create a new organization.
